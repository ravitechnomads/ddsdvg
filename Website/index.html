MILLENABOTS

 Millenabots is an NFT collection of 4K resolution, 3D rendered images, created by a group of millennials from London, UK. As a team we wanted to create an art style symbolic of who we are and where we want to be. We decided the best way to show this is by paying homage to nostalgic elements of our childhood, fusing it together with cyberpunk & futuristic themes.

Millenabots is a celebration of our past, present, and an auspicious anticipation of the future. Our past meets our future and our future meets our past.

JOIN THE PROJECT

7777 Millenabots are coming together to create a top-tier alpha community in the Solana space, offering web3 education, NFT & crypto alpha for passionate NFT collectors, with grand plans for their future in the web3 space. Holders will have FREE access to phoenix.tools, a brand spanking new NFT tracking & analytics platform, bringing holders the latest and very best trading tools in the space. Our team is also big on climate change, so we’re going to be donating 10% of royalties from secondary sales to planting trees across the globe.

Roadmap

Phase 1
Alpha Group
We’ve built a network of experts in the NFT space, cryptocurrency, e-commerce and growth mindset principles, to offer our discord community value not seen elsewhere! We’ve already started gifting our OG & whitelisted members with:
  • Web3 education
  • NFT alpha
  • Crypto Alpha

We’re committed to sharing value from DAY ONE!
Phase 2
Launch of the Collection
Our team is working hard to ensure each avatar is of the highest artistic quality, unique and created in 4K resolution. The artwork choice for the project includes millennial nostalgia with futuristic themes, to combine a celebration of our past, present, and an auspicious anticipation of the future.

We’re targeting mid July 2022 for the collection’s mint date on the Solana blockchain.
Phase 3
Climate Change
As Web3 builders, we’re determined to help reduce the harm of carbon emissions of blockchain technology applications. Therefore, we'll be donating 10% of all secondary sales' royalties to onetreeplanted.org to plant trees where they're most needed.

Our initial goal is to plant 100,000 trees - together we can make a difference on climate change!
Phase 4
Merch & Collabs
We want to bring our project’s philosophy of merging our past, present and future to create some unique merch, so we’ve partnered with a boutique streetwear brand based in London to help bring the vision to life. We’re also planning to airdrop our holders custom millenabots sneakers and 3D printed fashion accessories for you to show off in the physical world.

Our collabs team will continue working hard post-mint to build relationships with pop-culture brands & other blue-chip projects to propel brand awareness.
Phase 5
NFT Analytics Platform
We’ve started development of our NFT tracking & analytics platform, phoenix.tools!

All Millenabots NFT holders will have free access! The site will include live floor prices, volume & history on collections, wallet tracking analysis, NFT sniping, charts, custom alerts, curated feeds and more! A one-stop-shop for serious NFT investors. Premium payment plans will be available for non-token holders.
